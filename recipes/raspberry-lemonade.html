Easy Raspberry Lemonade

A Nice Drink For The Summer

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 fluid ounce) can frozen raspberry lemonade concentrate
  • 3 cups water
  • Â¾ teaspoon lime juice
  • 1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage
  • 1 cup crushed ice
  • 1 cup fresh raspberries, garnish
  • 18 Mint leaves, for garnish

Steps

  1. In a large punch bowl combine raspberry lemonade concentrate, water and lime juice.
  2. Then stir in lemon-lime soda and crushed ice.
  3. When serving garnish each glass with a raspberry and a mint leaf
